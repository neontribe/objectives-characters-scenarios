A "lightning talk"

Called "Objectives, characters and scenarios" - meaningful ideas out of the aether. A twenty minute lightning talk explaining how our bit of the industry starts to think about what a digital thing should do.

Objectives

What is the project for? Why does it need to be done? May need a bit of dissection.

Characters

...or personas. People, if you will. Users, in fact.

Scenarios

...or situations. Places in time and space where these characters find themselves with needs that interlock somehow with the objectives our customer has brought us.

Ideas

An idea is a post-it note sized thought for a product or feature - that's all.

Characters in scenarios lead to ideas.

That's it? Pretty much.

Pick a card...

Deal a "character" card, together with an "scenario", see what happens, re-deal if necessary.

Gimme 20...

10 ideas in five minutes, one in ten minutes...
